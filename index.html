Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish with bacon and eggs

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 6 oz bacon, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
